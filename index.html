Snake Game

Information

Snake is a sub-genre of action video games where the player maneuvers the end of a growing snake. The player must keep the snake from colliding with itself, which gets harder as the snake lengthens.

How to play:


By using the Arrow Keys, the player maneuvers the snake while consuming apples. Red apples will increase the level and the snake's length while Blue apples will decrease the level and keep the length.

Yellow apples will increase the snake's speed.

Difficulties:


Easy - the game is over only when the snake's head is touching itself.

Medium - the snake's speed is increased, also bad apples may occur.

Hard - the game is over by touching the edges of the board.
